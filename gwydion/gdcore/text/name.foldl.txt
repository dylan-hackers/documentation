Function: foldl
==============================

#foldr# in reverse. This function rebuilds a list by recursively applying a function over the list. For example:

> foldl(method(x, y) pair(factorial(x), y) end, #f, #(1, 2, 3, 4, 5))
>  â‡’ #(120, 24, 6, 2, 1 . #f)
