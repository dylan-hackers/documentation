Constant: $platform-name
==============================

A constant indicating the CPU and OS. This constant is set to the combination
of #$machine-name# and #$os-name#, for example, #"x86-win32" or #"powerpc-Darwin".


Exported from:
------------------------------
- #Module operating-system#


Type:
------------------------------
#<symbol>#


Value:
------------------------------
platform-specific
